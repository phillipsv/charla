BIO


Charla Lauriston is a Brooklyn-based stand-up comedian and television writer. She's written for Comedy Central's Why? with Hannibal Buress, Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt, the Tina Fey produced Netflix comedy, and TBS's People of Earth. Charla is the creator of the critically acclaimed original series Clench & Release. She’s performed at the New York Comedy Festival, the She-Devil Festival, and the Chicago Women’s Funny Festival, has been listed as one of 8 Black Women Ready for SNL by Essence Magazine, was Joke of the Week in TimeOut New York, and has been featured on MTV and Funny or Die.
